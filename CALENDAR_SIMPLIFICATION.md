# 🎨 Calendar & Timezone Simplification

## Overview
The calendar interface has been redesigned with a clean, minimal aesthetic - removing colors and focusing on simplicity and clarity.

---

## ✨ Changes Made

### 1. **Google Meet Logo** 📹
- **Larger & More Visible:** Increased from `w-5 h-5` to `w-7 h-7`
- **Better Icon:** Using the official Google Meet logo with proper proportions
- **Clear Label:** Added `font-medium` weight to make the text more prominent
- **Result:** The Google Meet branding is now crystal clear and professional

### 2. **Timezone Selector** 🌍
**Before:** Blue highlights, colorful hover effects
**After:** Clean, minimal design

- **Trigger Button:**
  - Simple gray text that turns white on hover
  - Smaller chevron icon (`w-3 h-3`)
  - Globe icon for better visual context

- **Dropdown Menu:**
  - Dark background (`#1a1a1a`) with subtle borders
  - **No colors** - just white/gray text
  - Selected timezone: Simple white text with `bg-white/5` background
  - Hover: Subtle `bg-white/5` with gray-to-white text transition
  - Clean separators between items (`border-b border-white/5`)

### 3. **Calendar Navigation** 📅
**Before:** Rounded buttons with blue accents
**After:** Minimal hover states

- **Month/Year Display:** Clean white text with semibold weight
- **Navigation Arrows:**
  - Gray (`text-gray-500`) by default
  - Subtle hover background (`hover:bg-white/5`)
  - Lighter gray on hover (`hover:text-gray-300`)
  - Smaller gap between buttons

### 4. **Calendar Days** 📆
**Before:** Blue selection and today highlight
**After:** Black & white design

- **Selected Date:** 
  - `bg-white text-black` - Clean white background with black text
  - Bold font weight for emphasis

- **Today's Date:**
  - `bg-white/10` - Subtle white tint
  - `ring-1 ring-white/20` - Thin white ring instead of blue border
  - White text, semibold

- **Available Dates:**
  - Gray text (`text-gray-400`)
  - Minimal hover (`hover:bg-white/5`)
  - Text brightens on hover (`hover:text-gray-200`)

- **Past/Weekend Dates:**
  - Darker gray (`text-gray-600`)
  - Cursor not-allowed
  - No hover effects

### 5. **Time Slots** ⏰
**Before:** Blue selected state, colored backgrounds
**After:** Clean minimal design

- **Selected Time:**
  - `bg-white text-black` - Clean white background, black text
  - Matches the date selection style

- **Available Times:**
  - Transparent background
  - Gray text with white border (`border-white/10`)
  - Subtle hover (`hover:bg-white/5`)
  - Text brightens on hover

- **Disabled Times:**
  - Transparent with barely visible border
  - Very dark gray text
  - No interactions

- **Spacing:** Tighter gaps between slots (`space-y-1.5`)

### 6. **Next Button** ➡️
**Before:** Blue gradient (`bg-blue-600 hover:bg-blue-700`)
**After:** Clean white

- `bg-white hover:bg-gray-100`
- Black text
- Consistent with the minimal theme

---

## 🎨 Color Palette

### Old Design:
- Primary: Blue (`#2563eb`)
- Hover: Darker Blue (`#1d4ed8`)
- Background: Dark grays with blue accents

### New Design:
- Primary: White (`#ffffff`)
- Secondary: Light gray (`#d1d5db`)
- Text: Gray scale (`#9ca3af`, `#6b7280`, `#4b5563`)
- Background: Pure black and dark grays
- Borders: Subtle white overlays (`white/5`, `white/10`)

---

## 🎯 Design Principles

1. **Monochromatic:** Black, white, and grays only
2. **Minimal Hover States:** Subtle `bg-white/5` instead of color changes
3. **Clear Selection:** White background for selected items
4. **High Contrast:** White on black for maximum readability
5. **Professional:** Clean, corporate aesthetic
